Welcome to the site!

About This Site

There are many online writing sites where people can compose written works, both fictional or non-fictional. Poetry is one of the types of writing on them, and this is a good place for it! Here, novice to experienced writers may put their free poetry here.

Get started by creating an account. You can log in anytime if you are registered.

What To Do

Posting poems is the first way a member can contribute to the site. On your user page during a session, you will be able to submit your poem. Include a title with text, and you are all set!

Reading poems is a varying experience, but haikus are currently the dominant work here. This site welcomes other types of short poems too such as free verse. View counters on other writing sites may cause larger works to outshine smaller ones... Thus, it was omitted for the sake of equal representation of poetry here.

Additional Information

This is a project done for the CSCI‐12 class at El Camino College. I made this to pay homage to free online writing sites. As for the code, it will be kept within a private GitHub repo, but the color palettes are free from colorpalettes.net!
